HANDS THAT HOLD

2025 | Performance

Credits

  • Choreographer & Performer: Mega Buana

Description

Hands That Hold foregrounds the concept of radical caring—a conscious, intentional practice of building consensual interpersonal relations. The choreography emphasizes bodily awareness, trust, mutual respect, and the willingness to share vulnerability. It explores how touch, weight, and presence can cultivate profound relational ethics.

Themes

Radical caring, embodiment, trust, vulnerability

KONSUALITAS

2025 | Choreographic Research

Credits

  • Choreographer: Mega Buana

Description

In this work, BDSM is approached not as provocation but as a critical framework for understanding power, boundaries, and radical care. The body becomes a site of negotiation—responding to pressure, distance, resistance, and emotional intensity. Konsualitas investigates fluidity between dominance and surrender, control and presence, revealing how vulnerability can coexist with strength.

Themes

Consent, power dynamics, embodied negotiation, BDSM as critical lens

LABYRINTH OF CONNECTION

2024 | Choreographic Research

Credits

  • Choreographer: Mega Buana

Description

This work delves into BDSM as a growing subculture and examines how communities form intricate networks of trust, communication, and shared identity. The use of rope is central—not merely as a prop but as a cultural design object shaped by collective practice. The work reinterprets rope as a metaphor for relational tension, structure, and interdependence.

Themes

BDSM subculture, social bonding, rope practice, design culture
